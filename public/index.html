BiotechData.Academy
Innovation in Data Analysis

At BiotechData.Academy we develop realistic and precise models of biotechnological processes. These models simulate complex experiments to speed up data analysis, test analysis workflows and improve data managment.

Advance Your Skills for Effective Biotech Data Analysis

We teach data analysis of various biotechnological experiments and analytical devices with realistic hands-on data from simulated experiments.

  • Learn different ways how to model growth experiments and how this matters in biotech
  • Get to know the analytics of metabolite measurements
  • Familiarize yourself with genome scale metabolic models, their construction and analysis
  • Maximize insight by combination of metabolic flux analysis and genome scale metabolic models

Improve your Data Managment with Realistic Data

At BiotechData.Academy we developed a digital biotech lab to simulate different experiments and analytical instruments. You can simulate as many experiments and data sets as you need to be confident of your laboratory and data managment software solution.

  • Basic biomass, substrate and product concentration measurements.
  • HPLC and Metabolomics analytics data.
  • Data from metabolic flux analysis with isotope label and flux balance analysis from metabolic models.
